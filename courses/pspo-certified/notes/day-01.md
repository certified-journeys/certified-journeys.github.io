# Day 1: Scrum Guide deep read #1

## Notes
- The scrum guide - rules of the game
- Light weight framework - helps people, teams and organizations manage complex projects.
- Needs: Scrum Master, Product Owner, Scrum Team.
- Purposefully incomplete; built on collective intelligence of people using it; rules of scrum guides relationships and interactions.
- Scrum Theory; founded on lean thinking; reduces waste and focuses on essentials; iterative and incremental; combines 4 formal events: The Sprint, Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, Sprint Restrospective, 
- Emphirical Scrum pillars: Transperancy, Inspection and Adaptation.
- Scrum Values: Commitment, Focus, Openness, Respect and Courage.
- Commitments: Clear Definition of Done

## Key takeaways
- Scrum Theory
- Scrum Pillars
- Scrum Values
- Involved People, Roles and Responsibilities.
